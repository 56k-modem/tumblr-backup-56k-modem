dreamfibre:

Terminator 2 made a huge impact on me as a teen because I found something particularly terrifying about it.

Indeed, the idea of a nuclear holocaust triggered by AI seems to gain more relevance by the day, bizarrely.

I think it’s this existential idea that separates it from what could have been just a great action movie. It’s a film that’s somehow much more than the sum of its (robotic) parts.

With my artwork, I wanted to explore the aesthetic Cyberdyne Systems, or moreover the version that exists inside my head. I always loved the steely blue tones and sinister, clinical feel. The almost benign facade to what is ultimately the factory of our extinction.

..Sleep well, everyone!! 🙃
